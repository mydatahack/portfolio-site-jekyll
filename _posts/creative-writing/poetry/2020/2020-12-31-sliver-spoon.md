Food fed by a Silver Spoon --

How Easy -- to spit it out --

How Easy -- to forget about it

And completely occupy Myself --

<br />
With -- a -- Thought --
<br />
<br />

How Good --

It could have been --

Had the Spoon been Gold
<br /><br />
