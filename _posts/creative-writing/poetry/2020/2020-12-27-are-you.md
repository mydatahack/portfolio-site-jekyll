title: Are you?
category: poetry

You are happy and whole.
<br /><br />
But,
<br /><br />
Are you looking for another weirdo --

to go on adventures

to go dancing?
<br /><br />
Someone who makes you laugh

And laughs at your jokes

Someone who makes you stand on your tippy toes

And kiss you at the right moment.
<br /><br />
Are you?
<br /><br />
Are you?
